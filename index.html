The Team

The Asset Management Bureau strives to help the Agency choose the right treatment for the right asset at the right time.

The Data Management Section is the technical branch of the Bureau.

The Role

We are seeking someone with strong technical skills to help us make spatial data more accessible to our users through lightweight web applications.

You will bring your considerable insights, inspirations, and innovations to enhance state government.
We will rely on your skills to help us find a better way of doing things. You could:
Invent a new way to use maps and data to drive better decisions. Code a web app. Make an infographic. Craft a cartographic masterpiece. Publish open data. Create an API.

What do you need?

The Skills

Frontend

Comfort with web technologies (JavaScript, HTML, CSS), especially working with REST endpoints and APIs to query and present data.

Web Mapping

Experience with web mapping libraries, such as Esri’s Javascript API, Leaflet or Mapbox.

Geospatial

Familiarity with geographic data. You’ll have plenty of support on this, but you should understand the strengths and limitations of spatial data, and how it can be modified.

Design

A good eye for design and an understanding of user experience principles.

Magic

You delight in the details. You enjoy spending the extra time to make the experience magical and continuously strive to raise the bar for your team.

Resourceful

You won't be asked to fly solo, but you should be resourceful enough to find solutions when you're stuck.

Self-Taught

You’ll be surrounded by teammates with deep experience in strategy, data, code, and design, but we expect to be learning just as much from you
