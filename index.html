Become Vermont's next Civic Hacker

The Vermont Agency of Transportation (VTrans) in Montpelier is seeking to hire a techincally savvy web mapper to join the Asset Management Bureau's Data Management Section.

The Team

The goal of Asset Management is to help VTrans identify the right treatment on the right asset at the right time.

The Data Management section aims to bring highway asset data to decision makers to enhance their processes. We support a wide range of business units with database management, reporting, analytics, and data collection. We often use custom web mapping applications to solve users’ problems.

The Role

We are seeking someone with strong technical skills to help us make spatial data more accessible to our users through lightweight web applications.

We are seeking a passionate web map developer to help make spatial data more accessible. You will be responsible for creating lightweight, user-friendly interfaces for our data. Bring your solid communication skills to bear on complex problems. Bring your empathy to stakeholder interviews and user testing. Come ready to learn, because our Agency possesses deep institutional, industrial, and transportation engineering knowledge. Bring your attention to detail to make our users’ experience magical.

The Tasks

What will you do on day one?

Mapillary Integration

We are partnering with Mapillary to host and serve our Right of Way forward facing photos, replacing the legacy system. We would like your help to bring our data into the ArcGIS platform with their integrations.

GeoCortex Development

Vermont uses GeoCortex to enhance our web based mapping applications. We would like you to pick up this pilot project and help take it to the next level.

VTransparency Support

VTrans is committed to transparency in all that we do. We're wrapping up a Scrum to develop the latest version of our Public Information Portal and are looking towards more advanced features in the future.
